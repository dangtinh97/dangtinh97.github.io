Privacy Policy

YouPiP Music

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

YouPiP Music ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user data.

2. No Account Required

The App does not require registration, login, or account creation. We do not collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

3. Data Collection

YouPiP Music does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No analytics tracking
  • No third-party advertising SDKs
  • No background data collection

4. Device Storage Access

The App may request permission to access your device storage. This permission is used only to scan and display audio and video files stored locally on your device.

Your media files are never uploaded, copied, or transmitted to any external server.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party services that collect user data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13.

7. Data Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit data, there is no external data storage involved. All media playback occurs locally on your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the official app store listing.
